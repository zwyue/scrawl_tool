
区别辨析mainly、chiefly、greatly、largely、mostly、primarily、principally与generally
2025年1月5日
口语与词汇


微信关注：田间小站 mainly、chiefly、greatly、largely、mostly、primarily、principally与generally这些副词均可表示“主要地、大量地”之意。
mainly : 指主要部分，突出在一系列事物中的相对重要性。


I mainly go to bed around midnight.
我通常午夜前后睡觉。
The group is made up of mainly young people.
该团体主要由年轻人组成。

chiefly : 侧重某人或某物在众多的人或物中占主要和显著地位。

The island chiefly attracts upmarket tourists.
这个岛屿所吸引的主要是高消费阶层的游客。
magazines intended chiefly for teenagers
主要面向青少年的杂志

greatly : 通常强调程度的高。

I greatly regret not having told the truth.
我非常后悔没有说出实情。
Her piano playing has greatly improved/has improved greatly.
她的钢琴弹奏大有进步。

largely : 着重范围或分量大大超过别的成分。

a largely male company
员工大多为男性的公司
Their complaints have been largely ignored.
他们的抱怨几乎没有引起任何重视。

mostly : 强调数量占多半，近乎全部。

In the smaller towns, it's mostly (= usually) very quiet at nights.
在小村庄里，晚上通常很安静。
The band are mostly (= most of them are) teenagers.
这支乐队的成员大多数是十几岁的青少年。

primarily : 侧重基本地，首要地。

We're primarily concerned with keeping expenditure down.
我们主要关心的是缩减开支。
Baseball is primarily a summer game.
篮球基本上是一项夏季运动。

principally : 侧重居首要地位。

The advertising campaign is aimed principally at women.
广告大战主要瞄准了女性。

generally : 通常强调普遍性。

Your health is generally good, but you do have a few minor problems.
总的来说你身体状况不错，但有些小毛病。
He wants more money to be given to the arts generally.
他希望各艺术门类都能得到更多的资金。
