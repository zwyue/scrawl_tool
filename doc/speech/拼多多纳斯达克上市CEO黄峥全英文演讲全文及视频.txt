微信关注：田间小站 7月26日晚，社交电商平台“拼多多”在美国纳斯达克正式上市交易，股票代码为PDD。此次拼多多IPO，同时在上海、纽约两地敲钟，这在纳斯达克历史上尚属首次。拼多多的创始人、董事长兼CEO黄峥用英文在上海发表了上市致辞，以下为全程视频及致辞全文：


Distinguished guests and honorable dear friends, good evening from shanghai, I am honored to take this precious opportunity to thank consumers in china and worldwide, because their every consumption is influencing and changing the landscape of technology, commerce and society.
For Pinduoduo, it's our 300,000,000 consumers and their families who brought us here, going forward, our consumer base will continue to grow no matter where theere is our consumers create value was always be our goal and focus, all along, we have been dedicated for enabling our consumers and their families to enjoy the convinience of mobile internet, to satisfy their needs, and at the same time, to have a happy shopping experience. This is exactly our new concept of new E-commerce.
Instead of focusing on products, we focus on better understanding of our users, instead of benefiting a small group of people, we serve the general public.
We strive to transform online shopping into a brand new experience which involves a lot more than offering, a lot more fun and happiness, i hope that our new E-commerce model will also improve the efficiency and quality of the conventional supply chain, one good example would be in angriculture industry, we find the team purchase, effective solution to angregate the consumer demand matches them with the angriculture produce, it allsow the necessary cost of the angricultural consumption and potentially make small scale costomize available, the social impact and the value of our pinduoduo bring to our society is much more business success or perceived valuation of the company.
Starting from today, Pinduoduo will become a public company, for my team and myself, we always stick to our core value of Benfen, as of today, our platform is just 3 years old, there is still a long ways to achieve,it's a long-term success, but i believe as long as our consumers, our merchants, our shareholders continues to trust us, as long as we dedicate ourselves doing right things and adhere it firmly to our core value, Pinduoduo's future will be bright and full of potential.
I am very proud to witness this moment, together with our consumers, our investors, distinguished guests and media friends, right now at this very moment, there is one family in shanghai tower and one consumer in New York ready to press the button for Pinduoduo to list in Nasdeq, this family in shanghai and consumer in New York will represent over 300 million consumers and their families who have trusted and supported pinduoduo all along, now let's start to write the beautiful journey.
